My Work

In addition to my academic position, I run Spotlight Media, producing video, web and design content for corporate clients including Willmott Dixon, the NHS, eat:Festivals and Birmingham Crane Hire.

Here are some of my recent freelance projects:

Eat Festival Promotional Video

Produced in collaboration with my business partner, this promotional video was created within government guidelines and restrictions, and was delivered to the client within 24 hours (including the animated introduction).

Nettle and Rye Promotional Video

This promotional video was shot on the Sony C100 and produced in collaboration with my business partner. It was delivered to the client within 24 hours of completion of the shoot.

RateMyFilm.co.uk

Developed using Javascript and the React framework, I developed and launched RateMyFilm so that film students could gain a deeper understanding of how age classifications are considered and awarded by the BBFC. This project also calls upon the IMDB API to dynamically access film data

Yoga Nurse Branding and UI Design

I designed this brand identity and user interface for Yoga Nurse, a venture launched by Megan Paxton (a nurse and yoga teacher located in Bristol). While the project itself has been postponed due to the recent pandemic, Megan intends to launch in late 2021.

More of my design work can be seen on my Behance profile.

Read, Re-Tweet, Revolt: An Investigation Into Social Media As Agents of Socio-Political Change

My MA thesis from the University of Gloucestershire investigates the role of social media during times of social-political upheavel, and as part of this project I interviewed dissidents across the Middle East and North Africa.
